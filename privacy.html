Day.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026 · Feather Studio

Day. is built around a simple principle: your data belongs to you. This policy explains what we collect, what we don't, and how your information is handled.

What we collect

Nothing. Day. does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. There is no account, no sign-in, and no analytics platform.

All data you create in Day. — tasks, field notes, voice recordings, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's local storage APIs.

Voice recordings and transcription

Day. includes a voice recording feature. When you record a voice note, the audio is processed entirely on your device using Apple's built-in speech recognition framework (SFSpeechRecognizer / SpeechAnalyzer). No audio data is sent to Feather Studio or any third-party server.

Apple may process a portion of audio on its own servers as part of its speech recognition service, subject to Apple's own privacy policy. Day. has no control over or access to this processing.

Local storage

Your tasks, notes, areas, and preferences are stored using @capacitor/preferences on your device. Audio files are stored in your device's local cache directory. None of this data leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

Export and import

Day. provides an export feature that creates a file containing your tasks, notes, and optionally your voice recordings. This file is created on your device and shared only through iOS's standard share sheet — to destinations you choose (Files, AirDrop, Mail, etc.). Feather Studio never receives a copy of your exported data.

Third-party services

Day. uses RevenueCat to process in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect purchase-related information (such as transaction IDs) as part of this process. Please refer to RevenueCat's privacy policy for details.

Day. does not use any advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or crash reporting services.

Children's privacy

Day. is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of Day. after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
