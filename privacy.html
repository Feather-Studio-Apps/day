Privacy Policy

Last updated May 17, 2026

Day. is built with privacy as a core principle. Your data belongs to you and stays on your device.

What we collect

Day. stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Intentions and tasks you create
  • Journal entries you write
  • Thoughts you capture
  • Areas you define
  • App settings and preferences

Where your data lives

All data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your data is synced to your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit framework. Daren Irlen never has access to your iCloud data — it goes directly between your devices through Apple's infrastructure.

What we do not do

  • We do not collect any data on our servers
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not show ads
  • We do not sell or share your data with anyone
  • We do not have access to your intentions, journal entries, or thoughts

Deleting your data

You can delete all your data at any time from the Profile tab in the app. This removes all local data from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, CloudKit data will also be deleted.

Children's privacy

Day. is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
